Calorie Calculator

Being Happy and staying active are very important. This calorie calculator assists you by calculating calories burned based on the amount of time you spent walking, assuming that you are going a brisk and steady pace without stopping. Remember, these calculations are estimates and used to best help you plan your day and track calories so that you can reach your fitness goals.
